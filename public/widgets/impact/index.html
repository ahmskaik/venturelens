VentureLens Impact Widget

Embeddable live KPIs from /api/v1/impact.json. Refreshes every 60 seconds. Use on partner sites, Devpost, or judge evidence pages.

Embed snippet

Replace the base URL with your deployment (e.g. https://venturelens.app).

<link rel="stylesheet" href="https://venturelens.app/widgets/impact/widget.css">
<div id="venturelens-impact"></div>
<script
  src="https://venturelens.app/widgets/impact/widget.js"
  data-base-url="https://venturelens.app"
  data-target="venturelens-impact"
  data-theme="light"
  data-refresh="60"
></script>
